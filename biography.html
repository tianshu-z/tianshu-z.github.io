BIOGRAPHY

My academic background is quite interdisciplinary, encompassing both life sciences and theatrical arts. I currently work in the field of cultural and artistic production, creation, and management, and have been consistently exploring the intersection of technology with art, media, and culture. My work is highly comprehensive in nature, but one of my core focuses is leveraging cutting-edge technological approaches to support meaningful cultural and artistic expression, creating cultural and artistic works that resonate with and are beloved by the people.

我的学术背景挺丰富的，包括生命科学和戏剧艺术。目前的领域是文化艺术制作、创作、与管理，我一直以来都在探索科技与艺术/媒体/文化的交集。 我的工作具有高度的综合性，但我在其中的核心关注点之一是使用前沿科技手段来支持有意义的文化和艺术表达，创作出人民群众喜闻乐见的文化艺术作品。
